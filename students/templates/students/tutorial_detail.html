Learner's Driving Test - Practice Questions

Test your knowledge with these 20 practice questions for your learner's driving test.

Question 1

What should you do when approaching a yellow traffic light?

A. Speed up to get through the intersection
B. Stop if it is safe to do so
C. Ignore it if no other vehicles are present
D. Honk your horn to alert other drivers
Correct Answer: B. Stop if it is safe to do so. If you cannot stop safely, proceed with caution.
Question 2

When may you legally exceed the speed limit?

A. When overtaking another vehicle
B. When driving on a highway
C. When driving late at night with no traffic
D. You may never legally exceed the speed limit
Correct Answer: D. You may never legally exceed the speed limit, regardless of circumstances.
Question 3

What does a solid white line along the right edge of the highway indicate?

A. The edge of the road
B. A bike lane
C. A pedestrian walkway
D. A passing zone
Correct Answer: A. The solid white line marks the edge of the road and the boundary of the driving lane.
Question 4

What should you do if your vehicle starts to skid?

A. Apply the brakes firmly
B. Steer in the opposite direction of the skid
C. Steer in the direction you want to go
D. Accelerate to regain control
Correct Answer: C. Steer in the direction you want to go and avoid sudden braking.
Question 5

When driving in fog, you should use:

A. High beam headlights
B. Low beam headlights
C. Hazard lights while driving
D. No lights during daylight hours
Correct Answer: B. Use low beam headlights in fog as high beams reflect off the fog and reduce visibility.
Question 6

What is the proper following distance in good conditions?

A. 1 car length for every 10 mph
B. 2-second following distance
C. 3-second following distance
D. 5 car lengths at all speeds
Correct Answer: C. A 3-second following distance is recommended in good driving conditions.
Question 7

When approaching a school bus with flashing red lights, you must:

A. Slow down and proceed with caution
B. Stop at least 20 feet away from the bus
C. Pass slowly on the left
D. Change lanes and continue driving
Correct Answer: B. Stop at least 20 feet away from the bus and remain stopped until the red lights stop flashing.
Question 8

What does a flashing yellow arrow at an intersection mean?

A. You must come to a complete stop
B. You have the right of way
C. You may turn in the direction of the arrow after yielding to oncoming traffic
D. The light will soon turn red
Correct Answer: C. You may turn in the direction of the arrow after yielding to oncoming traffic and pedestrians.
Question 9

What should you do if you have a tire blowout while driving?

A. Brake hard immediately
B. Grip the steering wheel firmly and gradually slow down
C. Turn quickly to the side of the road
D. Accelerate to maintain control
Correct Answer: B. Grip the steering wheel firmly, maintain your direction, and gradually slow down before pulling safely off the road.
Question 10

When are you required to turn on your headlights?

A. Only at night
B. From sunset to sunrise
C. When visibility is less than 1000 feet
D. From sunset to sunrise and when visibility is poor
Correct Answer: D. Headlights are required from sunset to sunrise and any time visibility is poor (fog, rain, snow, etc.).
Question 11

What should you do when an emergency vehicle with flashing lights approaches?

A. Speed up to get out of the way
B. Pull over to the right and stop
C. Continue at the same speed
D. Change lanes
Correct Answer: B. Pull over to the right edge of the road if possible and stop until the emergency vehicle has passed.
Question 12

What does a diamond-shaped sign indicate?

A. Stop
B. Yield
C. Warning
D. No passing zone
Correct Answer: C. Diamond-shaped signs are warning signs that alert drivers to potential hazards ahead.
Question 13

What is the proper hand signal for a right turn?

A. Left arm extended straight out
B. Left arm bent upward at a 90-degree angle
C. Left arm bent downward at a 90-degree angle
D. Right arm extended straight out
Correct Answer: B. Left arm bent upward at a 90-degree angle indicates a right turn.
Question 14

When approaching a railroad crossing with no warning signals or gates, you should:

A. Maintain your speed and cross quickly
B. Slow down, look and listen for trains, then proceed with caution
C. Come to a complete stop in all cases
D. Honk your horn before crossing
Correct Answer: B. Slow down, look and listen for trains, then proceed with caution if no train is approaching.
Question 15

What is hydroplaning?

A. Driving through deep water safely
B. When tires lose contact with the road due to water
C. A specialized driving technique for wet conditions
D. Accelerating on wet pavement
Correct Answer: B. Hydroplaning occurs when your tires lose contact with the road surface and ride on a layer of water.
Question 16

What is the minimum following distance in adverse conditions like rain or fog?

A. 2 seconds
B. 3 seconds
C. 4 seconds
D. 6 seconds or more
Correct Answer: D. In adverse conditions, you should increase your following distance to at least 6 seconds or more.
Question 17

When parallel parking, your vehicle should be positioned how far from the curb?

A. 6 inches
B. 12 inches
C. 18 inches
D. 24 inches
Correct Answer: A. Your vehicle should be positioned no more than 6 inches (15 cm) from the curb when parallel parked.
Question 18

What does a solid yellow line in the center of the road indicate?

A. Passing is allowed
B. No passing zone
C. Construction ahead
D. Two-way traffic
Correct Answer: B. A solid yellow line indicates a no passing zone where passing is not permitted.
Question 19

What should you do if you miss your exit on a highway?

A. Stop and back up on the shoulder
B. Make a U-turn using the median
C. Continue to the next exit
D. Cross the divider to turn around
Correct Answer: C. Continue to the next exit and then find a safe way to return to your route.
Question 20

What is the meaning of a red X above a lane on a highway?

A. The lane is reserved for emergency vehicles
B. The lane is closed to traffic
C. The lane is for high-occupancy vehicles only
D. Construction is ahead in that lane
Correct Answer: B. A red X above a lane indicates that the lane is closed to traffic and you should not drive in it.
