FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

You can book a driving lesson by calling our office, using our online booking system, or visiting us in person. We recommend booking at least one week in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

For your first lesson, please bring your learner's permit or provisional license, proof of identity, and payment method. If you're under 18, we'll also need a parent or guardian's consent form.

The number of lessons varies depending on your prior experience and learning pace. On average, new drivers require 20-30 hours of professional instruction. After your first lesson, your instructor will provide a personalized recommendation.
